IdlePlanetMinerTools.crafting_plan — Method
crafting_plan(inv::Inventory)

Appliies Recipies to the ~Inventory` until there are no negative item counts or there is no known recipie to resolve the current shortage.

Returns a list of CraftingActions and the final Inventory.
